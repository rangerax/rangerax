Who We Are

Built For
The Range

At RANGERAX, we design and manufacture premium timber target solutions purpose-built for professional shooting ranges. Our versatile and durable target racks are ideal for static long-range and close-range shooting, as well as dynamic tactical firearms training.

Each target unit combines robust construction with practical mobility. Our innovative design enables range operators to reconfigure entire layouts within minutesadapting to varying group sizes and training scenarios without compromising stability or safety.
