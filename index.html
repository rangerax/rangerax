About RANGERAX

At RANGERAX, we design and manufacture premium timber target solutions, purpose-built to meet the rigorous demands of professional shooting ranges. Our versatile and durable target racks (RAX) are ideal for static long-range and close-range shooting, as well as dynamic tactical firearms training.

We also offer custom-designed target frame solutions tailored for clubs and commercial ranges.

Designed and built in Toowoomba, Queensland, each target unit combines robust construction with practical mobility. Our innovative design enables range operators to reconfigure entire layouts within minutes—adapting to varying group sizes and training scenarios—without compromising stability or safety.

Safety Advantages

Our timber target frames significantly reduce the risks of ricochets and metal fragmentation during close-range shooting. When bullets strike metal stands or frames, they can deflect unpredictably, creating hazardous angles and dangerous shrapnel.

Timber, by contrast, allows projectiles to pass through rather than deflect, improving overall range safety without compromising functionality.

  • Significantly reduced ricochet risk compared to metal target frames
  • Minimised hazard from metal fragmentation
